    • BEEFEATER

    Type: London Dry Gin

    Description: Beefeater is a classic London Dry Gin that has been produced in London since 1863. It is known for its traditional and juniper-forward style, capturing the essence of London's gin-making heritage. The name "Beefeater" is derived from the Yeomen Warders, or Beefeaters, who are the ceremonial guardians of the Tower of London.

    Flavor Profile: Beefeater Gin is characterized by a bold and juniper-driven flavor profile. The predominant taste is that of juniper berries, which is complemented by citrusy and floral notes. The botanical blend includes ingredients such as angelica root, coriander seeds, and Seville orange peel, contributing to a well-balanced and refreshing taste.

    Aroma: The aroma of Beefeater Gin is juniper-forward, with a crisp and clean scent. Citrus notes and a hint of spice from the coriander are also present, creating a vibrant and inviting bouquet.

    Color: Beefeater Gin is clear and colorless, as is typical for London Dry Gins.

    Signature Expression: Beefeater London Dry Gin is the flagship expression and the most widely recognized. It is a versatile gin that forms the base for many classic cocktails.

    Beefeater 24: Another notable expression is Beefeater 24, which includes additional botanicals such as Japanese sencha tea and Chinese green tea. This creates a more complex flavor profile with subtle tea and floral undertones.

    How to Enjoy: Beefeater Gin is well-suited for a variety of classic cocktails, including the iconic Gin and Tonic, Martini, and Tom Collins. Its robust juniper character makes it a favorite choice for those who appreciate a traditional and crisp gin flavor.

    Legacy: Beefeater has a long-standing legacy and is recognized as one of the quintessential London Dry Gins. Its consistent quality and classic style have contributed to its enduring popularity both in the UK and internationally.
