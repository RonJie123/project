    • BLACK LABEL

    Type: Blended Scotch Whisky

    Description: Johnnie Walker Black Label is a well-known and respected blended Scotch whisky that has gained international acclaim. It is crafted by master blenders using a combination of single malt and grain whiskies from various Scottish distilleries. The whisky is aged for a minimum of 12 years, contributing to its smooth and sophisticated character.

    Flavor Profile: Johnnie Walker Black Label is characterized by a balanced and complex flavor profile. It often exhibits notes of rich malt, peat smoke, dried fruits, and a subtle hint of sweetness. The aging process in oak barrels imparts a smooth texture and a touch of vanilla and oak.

    Aroma: The aroma of Johnnie Walker Black Label is typically smoky and slightly peaty, with underlying notes of caramel, toffee, and a hint of citrus.

    Color: The whisky has a deep amber or golden brown color, reflecting its maturity and time spent in oak casks.

    Finish: The finish is long-lasting and satisfying, with a harmonious blend of smokiness and the warmth of spices.

    How to Enjoy: Johnnie Walker Black Label can be enjoyed neat, on the rocks, or as a key component in various classic cocktails such as the Black & Ginger or the Rusty Nail. Its versatility makes it a favorite choice for both sipping and mixing.
