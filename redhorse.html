    • RED HORSE

    Type: Lager Beer

    Description: Red Horse Beer is known for its strong and robust character, making it one of the stronger beers available in the Philippines. It is a pale lager with a higher alcohol content compared to regular beers, giving it a reputation for being a beer with a kick.

    Alcohol Content: Red Horse Beer typically has an alcohol by volume (ABV) of around 6.9%, making it a strong beer compared to many other lagers.

    Flavor Profile: The flavor profile of Red Horse Beer is characterized by a slightly sweet maltiness, a noticeable hop bitterness, and a hint of fruitiness. The higher alcohol content contributes to a warming sensation and a more pronounced body.

    Aroma: The beer has a malty and grainy aroma, with subtle hints of hops. The aroma is generally straightforward, reflecting the bold and no-nonsense nature of the beer.

    Color: Red Horse Beer has a golden amber color, similar to many lagers, with good clarity.

    Packaging: It is often available in distinctive large bottles, such as the 500ml "Extra Strong" bottle, emphasizing its strength and bold flavor.

    Popularity: Red Horse Beer is widely popular in the Philippines and among Filipino communities abroad. It is often associated with social gatherings and is considered a go-to beer for those who enjoy a stronger and more robust beer experience.

    Pairing: It pairs well with a variety of Filipino dishes and is often enjoyed during celebrations, festivals, and casual get-togethers.

    It's worth noting that the beer market can vary, and different regions may offer variations or limited editions of Red Horse Beer. As with any alcoholic beverage, it's important to consume it responsibly.
